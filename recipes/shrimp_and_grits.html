Shrimp and Grits

Ingredients

  • 2C reduced-sodium chicken broth
  • 2C 2% milk
  • 1/3Cbutter, cubed
  • 3/4t salt
  • 1/2t pepper
  • 3/4C uncooked old fashioned grits
  • 1C shredded cheddar cheese
  • 8 thick sliced bacon strips
  • 1 lb uncooked medium shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1t cajun seasoning
  • 4 green onions, chopped

Directions

  1. In a large saucepan, bring broth, milk, butter, salt, and pepper to a boil.
  2. Slowly stir in grits.
  3. Reduce heat. Cover and cook for 15-20 minutes or until thickened, stirring occasionally.
  4. Stir in cheese until melted. Set aside and keep warm.
  5. In a large skillet, cook bacon over medium heat until crisp
  6. Remove to paper towel.
  7. Saute shrimp, garlic, and seasoning in bacon drippings until shrimp turn pink.
  8. Stir in crumbled bacon nad heat through.
  9. Serve with grits
